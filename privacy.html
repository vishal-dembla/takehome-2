Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

Welcome to the Take-Home Salary Calculator. Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how we protect your information when you use this free tool to estimate your take-home salary, CTC breakdown, or in-hand salary in India.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

When you use this calculator, your data stays with you.

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No salary data is saved.
  • No information is sent to servers.

All calculations happen in your browser, helping you securely estimate your monthly and annual in-hand salary.

2. No Cookies. No Tracking.

We do not use:

  • Cookies
  • Google Analytics
  • Tracking pixels or any advertising tools

This calculator is designed for simplicity and privacy.

3. Your Salary Inputs Stay Private

Whether you are calculating based on:

  • New Regime vs. Old Regime tax slabs
  • EPF, HRA, Rent deductions
  • Annual or monthly salary breakdown

Your inputs are processed directly on your device. They are never stored, shared, or transmitted.

4. No Accounts. No Emails.

You don’t need to create an account, share your email, or provide any personal details. This is a free tool, with no sign-ups required.

5. External Links

If this website links to any external resources (guides, blogs, tools), those sites have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them separately.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. The latest version will always be available here with the updated date.
