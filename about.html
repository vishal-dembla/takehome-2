About This Tool

The Take-Home Salary Calculator for India was created with one simple goal: To help you calculate your take-home salary, CTC breakdown, and in-hand pay clearly, quickly, and without confusion.

Having spent 19+ years in recruiting — working across startups, large enterprises, and executive search — I’ve seen how many people struggle with something as basic as understanding their salary. Between CTC structures, tax deductions, EPF, HRA, metro allowances, rent, and the old vs. new tax regimes, figuring out your monthly take-home salary often becomes more complicated than it should be.

This tool simplifies all of that. You input your details. The calculator handles the logic.

Built Using AI — Not Hidden Behind AI

This tool was built with the help of Google AI Studio and ChatGPT, combining the power of modern AI with practical, human-centered design. The goal was to create something useful, transparent, and free — without forcing you into an app, login, or subscription.

Everything runs on your browser. Your data stays with you.
